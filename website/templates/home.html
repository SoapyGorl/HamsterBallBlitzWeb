Description:

Hamster Ball Blitz is a physics-based platformer where you play as a hamster in a ball with a water jet and a grappling hook. You can push off surfaces with your water jet and pull yourself toward surfaces with your grappling hook.

Explore an open world with a Forest, Anthill, Sewer, and 12 sub areas that improve your water jet and grappling hook.

Hamster Ball Blitz offers an in-game timer for speedrunning support.

A first playthrough should take 2-3 hours.

Hamster Ball Blitz is played with a keyboard and mouse and is only available on Windows. Your computer should have at least a 2 GHz processor and 3 GB RAM.


Visit my YouTube channel!

Tools used:

Python 3.11 — Sole programming language

Pygame — Collisions, timing, controls, sounds

OpenGL — Rendering visuals

Aseprite — Drawing sprites

MuseScore — Writing music

Audacity — Editing sound effects
